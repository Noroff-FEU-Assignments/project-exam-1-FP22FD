Hey guys!
Today’s free amigurumi crochet pattern is Snowman amigurumi from Designer Fernand’s Craft. So today you can learn how to make this beautifull amigurumi and have fun doing it.
Thank you to Fernanda’s Craft for providing and authorizing this pattern and the beautifull photos.

Skill level

intermediate. The approximate size of the toy depends on the yarn which we use. This pattern is written using American terminology and is worked in continuous rounds unless otherwise stated.

Abbreviations

MR = Magic ring
CH = Chain
ST = Stitch
SLST = Slip stitch
RND = round
INC = increase
DEC = decrease
SC = Single crochet
DC = Double crochet
BLO = back loop only
FLO = front loop only
* = repeats pattern
() = Parentheses are used for repeated patterns of stitches
[] = Stitch totals are shown in [square] brackets at the end of each round

Materials

1. Cotton yarn 50 g in Color Grey, Blue Green, White and Black
2. Crochet hook in accordance with the yarn you use (2.5 mm for all parts, except sweater that uses 3.5 mm)
3. Fiberfill / toy stuffing
4. 1 pair of Black safety eyes (6 mm)
5. Stitch markers

Head & Body

WHITE COLOR AND CROCHET HOOK 2.5 IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE YARN YOU USE.

Rnd 1: Start with 6 sc into a magic circle [6]
Rnd 2: Work 2 sc into each st around [12]
Rnd 3: (sc, inc) * 6 [18]
Rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) * 6 [24]
Rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) * 6 [30]
Rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) * 6 [36]
Rnd 7: (5 sc, inc) * 6 [42]
Rnd 8: sc around [42]
Rnd 9: (6 sc, inc) * 6 [48]
Rnd 10: sc around [48]
Rnd 11: (7 sc, inc) * 6 [54]
Rnd 12-17: sc around [54]
Rnd 18: (7 sc, dec) * 6 [48]
Rnd 19: (6 sc, dec) * 6 [42]
Rnd 20-21: sc around [42]
Rnd 22: (5 sc, dec) * 6 [36]
Rnd 23: sc around [36]
Rnd 24: (4 sc, dec) * 6 [30]
Rnd 25: (3 sc, dec) * 6 [24]
Rnd 26: (3 sc, inc) * 6 [30]
Rnd 27: (4 sc, inc) * 6 [36]
Rnd 28: (5 sc, inc) * 6 [42]
Rnd 29-35: sc around [42]
Rnd 28: (5 sc, dec) * 6 [36]
Rnd 29: (4 sc, dec) * 6 [30]
Rnd 25: (3 sc, dec) * 6 [24]
Rnd 25: (2 sc, dec) * 6 [18]
Rnd 25: (sc, dec) * 6 [12]
Rnd 25: 6 dec [6]

Nose

ORANGE COLOR AND CROCHET HOOK 2 mm IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE YARN YOU USE.

Rnd 1: Start with 6 sc into a magic circle [6]
Rnd 2: Work 2 sc into each st around [12]
Rnd 3: (sc, inc) * 6 [18]
Rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) * 6 [24]
Rnd 5-7: sc around [24]
Rnd 5-7: 20 sc, (inc) *4 [28]
Rnd 5-7: 24 sc, (inc) *4 [32]
Sew it between eyes.

Scarf

YELLOW WOOL COLOR AND CROCHET HOOK OF 4 mm mm IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE YARN YOU USE.
leave long tail at beginning to use later for sewing.
Start with Ch 7.

Row 1: hdc in 2rd ch from the hook and each ch to the end (6)
Row 2: ch 1, turn, hdc in blo of each stitch to the end (6 hdc)
Row 3-25: repeat row 2 (6 hdc)

Finish off and cut yarn leaving long tail for sewing.
Fold so that 2 short ends are matched up and stitch a seam along short end.

Hat

START WITH LIGHT GREY COLOR AND CROCHET HOOK 2.mm OR IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE YARN YOU USE.

Rnd 1: Start with 6 sc into a magic circle [6]
Rnd 2: Work 2 sc into each st around [12]
Rnd 3: (sc, inc) * 6 [18]
Rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) * 6 [24]
Rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) * 6 [30]
Rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) * 6 [36]

Embroidery hands and face

You can use a brown or black embroidery thread.
Insert the needle through the holes and emerge diagonally forming a half circle, pull the thread so that a knot disappears inside the body.
Look at the figures.
